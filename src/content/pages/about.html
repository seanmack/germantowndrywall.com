Our Legacy

In July 1954, Bill McEvoy and his young family made the life-changing move from Ireland to the United States. Quickly establishing himself in the drywall business, Bill soon founded his own company.

His son Kieran learned the trade from him and passed it on to his own sons. Today, McEvoy & Sons Drywall is a trusted name in the industry.

We've built a solid reputation over the years, thanks to our careful work and strong relationships with customers. We're proud to be a third-generation family business, and we hope to serve you for many years to come.

Bill McEvoy passed away in 2020, but his legacy lives on in our work. You can read more about our family's history in Irish Central and LaoisLive.

From day one, we've focused on stellar customer service. That's why our customers call us time and again. If you have a question or concern, you can speak directly to me any time. We pride ourselves on our fast, friendly customer care.

Kieran McEvoy Founder
