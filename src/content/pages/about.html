Our Legacy

In July of 1954, Bill McEvoy and his young family left Ireland and moved to the United States. Bill quickly found work in the drywall industry, eventually starting his own drywall company.

His son Kieran grew up in the drywall business, learning the trade from his father. Kieran taught his sons the same way. Today, McEvoy Drywall is one of the most trusted names in the industry.

Over the decades, countless satisfied customers can attest to the meticulous care and precision we bring to every project. Our reputation as a third-generation family business stands not only on the impeccable quality of our work but also on the trust and integrity we've fostered with our clients.

Bill McEvoy passed away in 2020, but his legacy lives on in our work. You can read more about our family's history in Irish Central and LaoisLive.

From day one, we've focused on stellar customer service. That's why our customers call us time and again. If you have a question or concern, you can speak directly to me any time. We pride ourselves on our fast, friendly customer care.

Kieran McEvoy Founder
